Activites

Activity #1: Local Produce & Drinks

Many colorful apples from your local farmers, freshly made apple cider, apple cider donuts, delicious food from food vendors, wines, wine tasting, are only some of the food and drinks you will see at the festival! Please be prepared to pay in cash and be aware of the lineups!

Activity #2: Photo Opportunities

Take pictures around the streets of Downtown Ithaca! There are lots of pretty backgrounds and murals on walls where you can take pictures with your family and friends!

Activity #3: Live Performances

Get ready for the cutest or the most fascinating performaces! This includes live music, live acts, and animal showcases. The schedule can be found here.

Activity #4: Clothing & Accessories

After getting stuffed from all the food, make sure to check out the shops on the streets, like handmade clothing and accessories!

Activity #5: Games

Play some games and arcades set up in the festival! Win prizes! Or take pictures in front of it for a cute background like I did!
